# v0.11.0 — 2026-07-07

The interactive-diplomacy release: AI civilizations now negotiate deals turn by turn, and you can steer a civilization yourself in-game.

## Diplomacy & Deals
- AI leaders now hold live, turn-by-turn negotiations through a dedicated negotiator, rather than one-shot offers.
- Redesigned deal screen with a central offer, side inventories, and clearer accept/reject cards.
- Deals show promise durations and richer inspection, with stronger checks and clearer error messages.

## Playing as a Human
- New in-game control panel lets you guide a civilization by choosing its flavors and persona.
- Right-click any option to jump straight to the relevant CivPedia entry.

## Under the Hood
- Support of Claude Agent SDK to allow for subscription-based consumption (please honor their usage policy!)
- Sessions can now pause and resume, and recover cleanly from dropped messages.
- Faster, cheaper turns thanks to improved prompt caching and clearer "reasoning tier" model settings.

## Not Yet Done
- Human control currently adjusts a civilization's flavors only — full strategy control is still to come.
- Some deal promises are temporarily disabled because the underlying AI doesn't yet act on them.
- The strategist orchestrator (autonomous multi-agent play) is designed but not yet implemented.
